VISITOR BADGE
  • Photo ID is required for admittance into the building
  • Acceptable forms of ID include: Driver's License, Passport or State ID.
  • A visitor badge must be worn at all times while on FCA property.
  • Visitor badges are issued upon entry and to be returned during sign-out process when exiting the building.
  • Visitors must be escorted by an FCA employee at all times and are not allowed to tour the building unaccompanied.
CAMERAS / CAMERA PHONES
  • The use of photo imaging devices is strictly prohibited
  • FCA reserves the right to affix a temporary sticker over any camera phone lens.
EMERGENCY PLANS / SIGNALS
  • Site emergency plans are posted throughout the complex
  • Fire/Evacuation: Short repeating beeps.
  • Tornado/Take Shelter: Repeating siren tone.
  • All Clear: Long continuous siren.
SAFETY INFORMATION
  • Be careful of vehicles operating on the first floor, walk close to the wall, and no cell phone use while walking.
SMOKING
  • Smoking is prohibited at all times in the building.
  • There are designated smoking areas outside the building.
GUEST WiFi
  • Wireless Internet is now available throughout the building. If you plan on using this service, please ask a lobby representative for login credentials.
HELP DESKS
  • There help desks located throughout the building.
  • Help desk representative can provide assistance with directions, class schedules, and any other logistical questions.
PARKING & SHUTTLES
  • All MatchMaker participants and required are to park at the Pontiac Silverdome located at 1200 Featherstone Road, Pontiac MI 48342.
  • Please enter through Gate 5 - West Parking Lot.
  • Complimentary shuttle service is provided from 7:15 a.m. - 5:00 p.m.
